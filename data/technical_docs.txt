Topic: Slack App Scopes
Required Scopes for RAG Bot:
1. app_mentions:read - To see when people talk to the bot in channels.
2. chat:write - To allow the bot to send messages.
3. im:history - To read and respond to direct messages.
4. groups:history - (Optional) To read messages in private channels.
5. channels:history - (Optional) To read messages in public channels.

Common Issues:
- "Bot not responding": Usually missing Event Subscriptions or Socket Mode not enabled.
- "403 Forbidden": The bot was added to a channel but lacks permissions to post.
- "Invalid Token": Mismatch between xoxb (Bot Token) and xapp (App Token).
